# E-Commerce Sales Dashboard (Excel Project)

## Overview
This project presents an **interactive E-Commerce Sales Dashboard** built using **Microsoft Excel**.  
It analyzes sales data to uncover trends in **customer behavior, regional performance, and sales channels**.

The dashboard enables quick decision-making by converting raw data into **clear visual insights**.

---

## Objectives
- Compare **Orders vs Sales performance**
- Identify **peak sales months**
- Analyze **customer demographics (Gender & Age)**
- Evaluate **order fulfillment status**
- Identify **top-performing states**
- Analyze **sales channels contribution**
- Determine **top product categories**

---

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel  
  - Pivot Tables  
  - Pivot Charts  
  - Slicers (Interactive Filters)  
  - Data Cleaning

## Key Insights (From Dashboard)

### Orders vs Sales Trend
- Sales peaked around **March (~1.92M)** with highest order volume (~2900 orders)
- After March, a **gradual decline trend** is observed till November  
- Slight recovery observed in **December**
👉 Indicates strong **Q1 performance and mid-year slowdown**

---

### Gender-Based Sales
- **Women contribute 64% of total sales**
- **Men contribute 36%**
👉 Female customers are the **primary revenue drivers**

---

### Order Status
- **92% orders are successfully delivered**
- Cancelled: 3%  
- Returned: 3%  
- Refunded: 2%  
👉 Shows **high operational efficiency** with minimal losses

---

### Top Performing States (Sales)
1. **Maharashtra – 2.98M (Highest)**
2. **Karnataka – 2.65M**
3. **Uttar Pradesh – 2.10M**
4. Telangana – 1.71M  
5. Tamil Nadu – 1.68M  
6. Delhi – 1.27M  

👉 Revenue is **highly concentrated in top 3 states**

---

### Age & Gender Analysis
- **Adult women (~35%)** contribute the highest orders
- Adult men (~15%) are significantly lower  
- Teenagers and seniors contribute less comparatively  
👉 Target segment = **Adult female customers**

---

### Sales Channel Contribution
- **Amazon – 35% (Highest contributor)**
- Myntra – 23%  
- Flipkart – 22%  
- Ajio – 6%  
- Meesho – 5%  
- Others – 4%  
- Nalli – 5%  

👉 Amazon dominates → **primary sales channel**

---

### Business Insights Summary
- Focus marketing on **women (especially adults)**
- Invest more in **Amazon & Myntra channels**
- Strengthen presence in **Maharashtra & Karnataka**
- Improve performance during **mid-year sales dip**
- Maintain high delivery success rate
